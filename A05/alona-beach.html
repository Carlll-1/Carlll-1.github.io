Alona Beach

Experience the thrill of jet skiing on the crystal-clear waters of Alona Beach.

Adventure Awaits

Alona Beach is a paradise for adventure seekers and beach lovers. Enjoy water sports, relax on the white sands, and explore the vibrant marine life.

Highlights

  • Jet ski across the turquoise waters.
  • Snorkel and dive to see colorful coral reefs.
  • Relax on the pristine white sand beaches.
  • Enjoy fresh seafood at beachfront restaurants.
